Spark.noConflict()

Parameters

None.

Returns

Returns an instance of the Spark object.

Examples

Revert the $ variable back to how it was before Spark arrived.

Spark.noConflict();

Revert the $ variable but reinitiate it under a different alias.

window.$S = Spark.noConflict();
